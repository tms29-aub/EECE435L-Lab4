A class representing a course.

This class defines attributes and relationships associated with a course, including its instructor and enrolled students.

Parameters:

  • course_id (int) – Unique identifier for the course, auto-incremented.

  • course_name (str) – The name of the course.

  • instructor_id (int) – The identifier of the instructor teaching the course.

  • instructor (Instructor) – A relationship to the Instructor object teaching the course.

  • enrolled_students (list of Student objects) – A relationship defining the students enrolled in the course.
